on coding but also on developing and encouraging positive soft
skills such as empathy, kindness, mindfulness, etc.

of time and effort and good preparation is key!

I see myself fitting in quite well in this type of culture.

and going forward we help each other where we can.

No. I still expect to put in a lot of effort and work as a team!
