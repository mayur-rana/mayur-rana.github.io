What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

I think it is a great approach where the focus is not solely on coding but also on developing and encouraging positive soft skills such as empathy, kindness, mindfulness, etc.

What are your impressions?

My impressions are, overall, positive. This will take a great deal of time and effort and good preparation is key!

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I see myself fitting in quite well in this type of culture. Individually we will all bring different experiences and qualities and going forward we help each other where we can.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

No. I still expect to put in a lot of effort and work as a team!

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

I'm very excited and slightly nervous beacuse there is still some fear that I won't be prepared enough by the time bootcamp starts!
