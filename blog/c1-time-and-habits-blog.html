which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I tried out the Fogg method.

what did you find interesting?

I found that rather than having a big to do list or having massive goals, starting with some small habits can be very positive to being productive and getting things that matter done.

what worked for you?

Simply getting started can be quite hard at times! I found (step 3) triggering the behaviour to be a great help. In my case after everytime that I go to the bathroom (to wash my face, brush my teeth, shower, etc), I do a chunk of work on the sprints. I also do the same after I have a meal or a snack.

which will you experiment further with?

I will definitely be trying out the Pomodoro Technique further.
